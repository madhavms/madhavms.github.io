About

I'm a software engineer on a commodities risk & pricing team, turning messy, high-volume market data into the market-risk and reporting systems that risk managers and traders rely on.

Across six years I've built and owned the pipelines behind market-risk (VaR) and energy-market platforms — from consolidating end-of-day and intraday feeds out of 8+ source systems into a single position store that feeds all downstream risk reporting, to standardising heterogeneous vendor feeds into one canonical view for a power desk. I care most about production reliability: root-causing and fixing live pipeline issues end-to-end, backed by reconciliation and diagnostics that gate the risk report each morning.
